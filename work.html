Liana's Piano Lessons

Challenge
This website is a real project that I worked on to create a website for my private piano lessons. The objective is to capture main information about lessons and let visitors book a lesson. The target audience is parents (25-60 years old) who want to book lessons for their children and adults (16-50 years old) who wish to have piano lessons.

Solution
I approached this project in mind with something to stand out from competitors, I have found that their websites look outdated and lack creativity, considering that piano is a form of art. I added abstractness by making a note appear big on the first screen, immediately catching attention, and asking a question 'want to play piano?'. Call to action is present on every block and is relevant to the displayed information.

Bentley Flying Spur

Challenge
Create a landing page to feature new Bentley Flying Spur. It is important to capture car's style in the design - luxury and sporty.

Solution
I created a color palette based on the colors of the car, exterior and interior. The design reflects Bentley's luxury style with an elegant font and stylistic elements, such as large typography on the background, giving the website uniqueness.
