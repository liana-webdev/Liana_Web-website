Website Design

Before a house is built, we hire an architect and designers to map things out. Likewise, you need a web designer to design your website.

I offer such a service and it includes:

A custom and unique design that is easy to navigate for all devices and sizes, suited to your brand identity

A clear value proposition that tells visitors what they can expect from your website/product/service

Strong calls to action that encourage visitors to take the desired action and reach your goals

The approach

Interview

We conduct an interview to identify your needs and goals, understand more about your industry, research and analyse your target audience and competitors.

Wireframing

Creating your website structure in form of a diagram that will include blocks of your site with content description. From that, I create a wireframe reflecting the structure. Most revisions will be done at this stage.

Design

Converting wireframe into design in mind with responsiveness on desktop, tablet and mobile devices.

Hand-off

The end-result is a zip file containing:

Design source file (Figma)
High resolution images of the design + previews
All icons, photos and illustrations packed in their own folder

Add-on: Dynamic Prototype

While having a design reday to be developed, you want to bring it closer to reality - get an animated prototype. You need it for two reasons:

1. Establish interactivity

A prototype is like a power point presentation when things move around at a certain time or when you click on them. This is needed to visualize how your website will work and feel and serve as the main reference for the development stage, saving you a lot of time.

2. Decide what animations you want to have

Animations are needed to create more connection between the user and the website by entertaining them with smooth and meaningful dynamic elements. Animations make information easier to digest (if done right) and give a wow-effect to your design.




You need a dynamic prototype to decide what animations and interactivity you want to have before coding the website. As I am a web developer myself, I come up with unique and wow-like animations in mind with feasibility.

Developers will be very happy to see that you have decided how each animation will work, at what times and under what conditions, and how it will change. I will describe all that and you'll get a peace of mind knowing that when you hand it to a developer, he will know exactly what to do.
