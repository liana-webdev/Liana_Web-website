About me and what I do

Hey, I am Liana. I specialise in creating designs for websites.

Starting back in 2017, my journey has been full of constant education and work. I've been doing a lot of self-learning and that has really sharpened my research skills. However, I also have a web design certificate and web development diploma.

I used to create designs and websites for people, but that was just a side job. In mid 2022, I finally started working on this business full time - viola, it's been only a few months and this website is finally live! =)

So yeah, I know web design and development, graphic design, and marketing.

Having the power of these I am able to create design that is not only simple to use and looks great but effectively delivers the message and turns prospects into clients.

That’s when I started designing this very website.

Actual photo I took on that day from my office

And that’s me today ready to work with you.

My job isn’t just about making an aesthetically appealing and intuituve design - that stuff I do by default (and I do it good).

What I’m really focusing on is understanding you, your business and your goals.

Your values. Your client.

Why you do what you do and why others should care.

All this is to articulate your value proposition to make the design immensly powerful.

Alright, enough about me.

Because, really, it’s all about you.

I get it, your looks are indeed important.

Aesthetics and comfort matter. In design, we call it good visuals, user interface and experience.

I deliver you these features through custom design and unique approach to the project.

All of the above are vital for any business. Your customer's journey starts from the very first interaction they have with your brand, whether it's through your website, app, or brick-and-mortar store.

It's important to make a good first impression, as this will set the tone for the rest of their customer journey. If you can provide a smooth and seamless customer experience from start to finish, you'll be well on your way to acquiring and retaining loyal customers.

Furthermore, a positive user experience will also lead to increased conversions and sales.

Investing in an aesthetically pleasing and simple interface and user experience is one way to set yourself apart and give yourself a competitive advantage.

It’s also important to have your brand identity communicated through the design, because you obviously don’t want to blend in with others, right?

We want your visitors to do the “right” thing.

In order for this to happen, I have a strategy in place.

You and I will have put a lot of time and effort into your website.

You’d want visitors to stay awhile, browse around, and ultimately become clients.

It works through smart visual hierarchy of elements on your website, which include text, images and graphics.

With a thought-through design that I offer, visitors will perceive information quicker and easier, and be guided to become clients. This way you're giving your visitors the best possible chance to engage with your site and convert into clients.

I take give care for your funds.

I carefully determine all your needs and have a strategy in place to make sure your design is complete. This results in a lower cost for you overall.

Who wants any unforeseen costs?

A well-planned design for a website can result in lower development costs and avoid additional costs in the future.

In addition, should any changes need to be made down the road, they will likely be much less expensive than if no good planning had been done upfront.

So not only can you save money by having a well-planned design, but you can also avoid costly surprises down the road.

We all want it quick & simple.

I strive to create more intuitive websites that result in bigger sales and happier customers for your business. It's the right thing to do.

After all, who wants to waste their time searching for something on a website with bad design?

You want your visitors to be able to find what they're looking for quickly and easily, so they can be satisfied faster and move on with their lives.
